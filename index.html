About Me

I am a 6th-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Princeton University. I earned my Bachelor's degree at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research focuses on systems and networking, perticularly in 5G networks, congestion control, and network measurements for real-time applications, such as video conferencing.
